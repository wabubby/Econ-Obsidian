The attribute of pollutants of their dispersion.

Uniformally Mixing Pollutants (UM) are public bads.
- most greenhouse gases, methane, co2, etc.
- the location of the pollution is irrelevant
	- zoning policies have no effect, everyone gets a taste

Non-Uniformly Mixing Pollutant (Non-UM)
- location matters a lot
- nuclear waste, carbon monoxide, particulate polllutants, mercury, nitrogen and sulphur buildup
- these need zoning policies to sperate pollutants from potential victims
	- ahem ahem native americans having nuclear waste in their water table
